For me, You Are..

Out of all terms of endearment, why daffodil? Don't ask, just go with it. Char. Well, nakalagay kasi sa Google results na "Daffodils are associated with strength and resilience by virtue of their ability to survive and weather through winter storms and come out bright and cheerful in the spring." That's how I perceive you, a daffodil. That despite of all the hardships of life, you're still standing strong. Yes, you may be broken and shattered in pieces and just being held together by little hopes, but you're still standing. That's how I perceive you, a strong-willed person. It's just sad that you can't see it yourself, so please do know that that's you.

A Letter From Me To You


The letter I wrote in the sketch book was actually rushed. KSKSKS. So ayun, I just thought, why not make another one? And here I am. First of all, I can't find words to express how much I appreciate the trust and value you've given me. I even think I don't deserve any of that because I feel like I've put less effort into this friendship compared to you. But still, there you are, continuing to trust me. Thank you. I may not be so responsive at times, but please know that I am willing to listen to anything. I may not be there physically, but at least let me listen to you. I also want to tell you to please find some time to think of yourself too. I just noticed that you always put your problems before yourself. I know it's easier to listen to it than doing it, but consider doing that, okay? Don't be too hard on yourself. I know there are times you blame yourself for whatever is happening in your life. I know there are times you just want to end it all. I know and I understand, but please take care of yourself too. To be honest, I don't know how to respond to you whenever you confess to me about your thoughts about ending it. I don't know what I exactly should say. All I know is that the thought of losing someone you value is a nightmare. It's even more of a nightmare if you haven't gotten the chance to say everything you want to say to that person. I don't want to experience that again. hehe. You don't have to continue for anyone; continue for yourself. Keep on hoping. I know it's tiring, but at least those hopes still have the probability of coming true. I know it sounds so optimistic, but yeah. I have so much more to say, but as usual, I can't put it into words. Nevertheless, please know that you, too, are special to me. All of you are dear to me. Whatever happens in the future, I will still value this friendship. No matter how far we get from one another, I will never forget how this friendship shaped me as a person. Thank you as always. I wish you another year of finding joy and happiness.Â
